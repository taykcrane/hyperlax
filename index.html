Do you see those Instagram #hyperlapse videos on the left? They were just posted to Instagram; it's a real-time feed. Enjoy a peak into the lives of others, from locations around the world. Hyperlax is meant to be a bit of an escape, a bit voyueristic, a bit relaxing. I think it's mesmerising. Maybe you will too!

Hyperlax was created as a way for me (@taykcrane) to begin learning how to code. It was inspired by Hypermasher, a site with an almost identical concept, built by Andrew Baker (@andrewtorkbaker). I was drawn to Hypermasher, but felt like there were a few things I could do to make it better. And thus, Hyperlax was born!

If you've read this far, I'd honestly love your feedback. Positive/negative/neutral, feature requests/bug fixes, massive donations/angry rants can all be sent to taylorkcrane@gmail.com. I don't know if anyone is going to like this thing, so if you do, seriously let me know.
